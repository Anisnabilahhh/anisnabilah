My other half


My family has only 4 members. My father's name is Mohd Muliadi bin Mohd Nor. He was born on May 25, 1975 in Port Dickson, Negeri Sembilan. He is the third child of 5 siblings. My father is self-employed, a grab driver. My father's crafting attitude, going out to work 4 times a day made me continue to be diligent and enthusiastic in continuing my studies.

My mother's name is Norziah binti Jalil. She was born on 29 September 1975 in Petaling Jaya, Selangor. My mother is the sixth child of 7 siblings. She works as an Islamic bank officer, and my mother has been in this banking field for 28 years. My mother's perseverance and discipline make me continue to be enthusiastic and brave in completing the field I am currently engaged in. My mother is an incredibly gorgeous, friendly, compassionate, and kind person. My own mother taught me several things, one of which is that we should always share our food with the people and animals in the area around us. I constantly ask God to prolong my mother's life so that one day I can bring her joy and pride.
